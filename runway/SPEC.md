# Runway

Build mode: **MVP build**.
Build priority: **8**.

## App Name

Runway

## Category

Utilities

## One-Sentence Promise

Shows freelancers and irregular-income earners exactly how many days of runway they have left, without linking a bank account.

## Target User

Freelancers, contractors, and gig workers with irregular income who find steady-paycheck budgeting apps useless or overwhelming.

## Painful Moment Solved

Constant low-grade anxiety about how long you could coast if work dried up, with no simple, privacy-respecting way to see it.

## Why It Is Different

One core number — days of runway — projected from manually entered recurring and one-off entries, explicitly with no bank linking, a real privacy-first differentiator against account-linked competitors.

## Why Users Would Pay

Multiple what-if scenario projections, historical accuracy tracking of past projections, recurring-income templates for freelance patterns, tax-quarter export.

## Subscription Model

Free: single runway projection, manual entries. Paid: scenario modeling, historical tracking, export.

## Local-First MVP Scope

Fully on-device ledger of income/expense entries with recurring-entry support and runway calculation.

## Future Backend Roadmap

Optional cloud backup/sync; possible opt-in CSV statement import later, still never live account linking.

## AR / Local ML / Local LLM Fit

None — an honest, non-ML personal finance tool by design.

## Design Direction

Calm, confidence-inspiring — a single hero number as the centerpiece, muted premium palette, no alarmist red/green gamification.

## App Store Positioning

Know your runway — without linking your bank account to another app.

## Invalidation Criteria

If active logging drops off sharply after week 2, manual-only entry is unsustainable and needs an import or receipt-photo shortcut.

## v1 Build Scope

Entry logging with recurring support, runway calculation/display, one what-if scenario, historical log view.

## Core Workflows

- Add income/expense entries (one-off or recurring)
- View runway projection
- Model a what-if scenario
- Review historical accuracy of past projections

## iOS-Native Features

- Widgets showing current runway at a glance
- Live Activities for a runway countdown during a lean stretch
- App Intents/Siri for quick entry

## Key Risks

- Manual-entry discipline is the single biggest adoption risk
- Entry must take under 5 seconds or it won't stick
- Competing against free bank-linked apps requires the privacy pitch to be foregrounded clearly
