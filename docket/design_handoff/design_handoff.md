This changes the whole design vocabulary: every digest card needs a confidence/completeness indicator, not just a done/not-done state.

On state management: I'd go `@Observable` view models per feature (FollowingFeedModel, SourceDetailModel, AddSourceModel), backed by SwiftData for persistence of FollowedSource, Meeting, DigestItem, and DiffRecord entities. Import/processing work is genuinely long-running — summarizing an 80-page agenda on-device, even with Apple's on-device model APIs, is not a sub-second operation — so this must be modeled as a real background Task tied to the entity's lifecycle, not to the view's lifecycle. If I bind that Task to `.task {}` on the detail view, and the user backs out mid-processing, per the pitfalls doc that task gets cancelled and the resident loses their place — bad, because they just triggered a 20-second job for nothing.
