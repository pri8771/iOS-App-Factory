The core loop is: open app at the gym session, start capture/import in one quick path with no mandatory pre-selection, let analysis run on-device at fixed low FPS for reliability, land in review with a route-overlaid trace and confidence readout, add quick outcome labels (send/fall and optional note), attach route metadata if needed, then compare against the last attempt on that route through a synced dual-attempt timeline that surfaces phase-by-phase deltas in plain language. Subscription value must be hard-gated and testable: free users hit an explicit local cap on stored analyzed attempts, paid users remove that cap and receive comparison mode and repeated weakness extraction history. The wedge is deterministic local pose sampling plus immediate visual + textual decomposition of where movement drifted, not just a one-box “AI score.” The most important design decision is this: competition is won by trust and speed, not by claiming precision the model can’t consistently provide.
