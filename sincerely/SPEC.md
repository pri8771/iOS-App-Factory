# Sincerely

Build mode: **MVP build**.
Build priority: **2**.

## App Name

Sincerely

## Category

Social Networking

## One-Sentence Promise

Helps you notice and say the specific, real thing you appreciate about someone — and actually get it to them.

## Target User

Emotionally intentional adults who want to strengthen relationships with family, friends, or mentors but lack a designed way to express it beyond generic texts.

## Painful Moment Solved

Thinking 'I should tell them how much that meant to me' and never doing it because there's no specific format, or it feels shallow over text.

## Why It Is Different

Solo-first structured gratitude journal that composes a specific-appreciation note and delivers it via native Share Sheet — the recipient needs zero app to receive it.

## Why Users Would Pay

Premium card designs/typography, relationship reminders ('you haven't told X anything in 6 months'), a private permanent archive/export.

## Subscription Model

Free: basic card style, manual sending, 5 saved relationships. Paid: unlimited premium designs, relationship reminders, archive/export.

## Local-First MVP Scope

Fully local journal and relationship reminders; sending via native Share Sheet rendering a shareable card, no server needed.

## Future Backend Roadmap

Optional cloud sync of the archive across devices; eventual two-way 'received' tracking if the recipient also has the app.

## AR / Local ML / Local LLM Fit

Optional on-device LLM writing assist to turn a rough thought into a well-worded specific note — opt-in, never auto-generating fake sentiment.

## Design Direction

Warm, tactile, fine-stationery aesthetic — letterpress textures and typography, not a social-app look.

## App Store Positioning

Say the specific thing. Sincerely helps you write and send real appreciation — not another generic 'thinking of you' text.

## Invalidation Criteria

If users send once and never return within 6 weeks with no engagement from relationship reminders, redesign the reminder mechanic before assuming the concept fails.

## v1 Build Scope

Relationship list, guided note composer, Share Sheet sending, one reminder type.

## Core Workflows

- Add a person
- Write a specific-appreciation note with guided prompts
- Preview a designed card
- Send via Share Sheet
- Browse personal gratitude timeline

## iOS-Native Features

- Share Sheet/Messages extension
- Widgets ('who haven't you thanked lately')
- App Intents/Siri for quick capture
- Local notifications for relationship nudges

## Key Risks

- Writing-assist overuse could feel like a greeting-card gimmick
- Specific-compliment prompts could feel like homework
- Premium card designs must not read as cosmetics-only monetization
