On overall shape: MVVM with `@Observable` view models, one per screen, already agreed — but I want to be specific about navigation because the design handoff's "crossfade, not push/pop" instruction for Recording→Processing→Summary has a real architectural consequence that nobody's stated yet. If Recording, Processing, and Summary are three separate `NavigationStack` destinations, SwiftUI's default transition is a push/pop slide no matter what modifiers you throw at it near the transition boundary, and back-swipe becomes a real hazard — a user mid-session could swipe-back out of Recording and orphan an active `AVAudioEngine` tap.
