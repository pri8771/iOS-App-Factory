# VerveCoach

Build mode: **MVP build**.
Build priority: **6**.

## App Name

VerveCoach

## Category

professional-communication

## One-Sentence Promise

Rehearse the conversations that matter before you walk into the room.

## Target User

Managers, founders, interview candidates, salespeople, and professionals who need to prepare for emotionally or financially important conversations.

## Painful Moment Solved

The user has one difficult conversation coming up and knows improvising will go badly.

## Why It Is Different

Rehearsal-driven, not a generic chat assistant or note pad; the product is about practicing responses under pressure.

## Why Users Would Pay

Better communication changes outcomes in interviews, negotiations, feedback conversations, and sales calls.

## Subscription Model

Free limited rehearsal scenarios; premium for unlimited sessions, custom scenario libraries, progress history, advanced coaching modes.

## Local-First MVP Scope

Scenario setup, role-based prompt cards, rehearsal flows, reflection notes, session history, optional on-device critique where available.

## Future Backend Roadmap

Cross-device sync, shared coaching templates, mentor review, richer speech analysis.

## AR / Local ML / Local LLM Fit

An on-device LLM is useful for critique and response variation, but the app ships strong rule-based rehearsal flows and fallbacks.

## Design Direction

Confident, minimal, psychologically safe with low-friction rehearsal cards and strong focus states.

## App Store Positioning

A private rehearsal app for people who need better outcomes from the conversations that matter.

## Invalidation Criteria

If the app is just a prompt box asking an AI what to say, it fails.

## v1 Build Scope

Local scenario rehearsal app with manual setup, guided response practice, audio capture, review notes, optional model-backed critique where supported, onboarding, paywall, persistence.

## Core Workflows

- Define a real upcoming conversation
- Rehearse likely turns and responses
- Review weak spots and next-best phrasing

## iOS-Native Features

- Speech recognition
- Audio recording
- App Intents for quick rehearsal starts
- Widgets for upcoming sessions
- Local notifications

## Key Risks

- LLM dependency must not become a device-eligibility trap
- Could drift into generic self-help if rehearsal structure is weak
